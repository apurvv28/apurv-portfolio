Most portfolio sites lean on a flashy color palette to create energy. This one takes the opposite route: the motion, contrast, spacing, and surface treatment have to do the work.

The result is a system that feels cinematic because every panel, light sweep, and shadow has a job. When the palette is restrained, composition becomes the actual visual language.

### What matters most

- clear hierarchy
- readable contrast on glass surfaces
- motion that supports the message instead of distracting from it

That constraint makes the interface feel deliberate rather than decorative.
