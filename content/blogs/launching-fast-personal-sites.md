A fast personal site is not just a technical achievement. It is a signal that the portfolio respects the user's attention.

The trick is to make every animation justify its existence. If a visual effect does not help the story, the navigation, or the sense of craft, it can usually be removed.

### A simple rule

Keep the structure light, the surface treatments consistent, and the content easy to scan.
